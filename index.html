What Is Our

Desired Outcome

Every product thrives from a vision.
A vision of what it will become. A vision of how it changes the future. A vision of how it creates identity with its users.

Every product must exist in reality
To understand where a product fits in the world you must understand its user. User research tethers a product and empowers a company with knowledge of where it exists in the world.

Every product creates an experience
UX design is the path that brings vision and research together to create a product that lives in the hearts and minds of its users.

Communicating User Experience

Strategies

UX Insights

Leveraging insights from research, psychology, and UX best practices brings a customer centric approach to helping an organization make strategy decisions, and acts as an agent of change for an organization.

UX Product Management

To be truely effective, a UX strategist must reach across an organization to get executive ownership, investment, and communication. They must think in terms of objectives and key results in order to measure and show progress forward.

UX Process

An ideal UX process includes these 6 steps:
1) Data collection
2) Persona creation
3) Design team ideation
4) Creating scenarios and user stories
5) Defining requirements
6) Wireframing and prototyping

UX Transformation

The main benefits a UX Strategist brings to a business is:
1) Assimilating customer data to guide design.
2) Ensuring the teams align their direction with their organization's business plan.
3) facilitating strategic, customer centric decision making.
4) reaching across the enterprize to build relationships that help the UX effort succeed.
