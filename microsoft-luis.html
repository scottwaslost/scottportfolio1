What is LUIS?

LUIS is the customizable natural language processing platform that sits in a suite of artifical intelligence APIs provided in Microsoft's Azure protal. If your app needs to recognize the intentions around user text or voice inputs, using this language modeling processor is one of the best choices out there.

Role of

Natural Language Processors (NLP)

Chatbot Understanding

NLP is the understanding side of your chatbots. When a user makes an inquery to your chatbot the NLP decides what the intention behind that question is by using a language model constructed beforehand. This model is trained on simular phrases a user may ask and can then identify new and novel questions.

Voice Commands and Interactions

Paired with a speech to text service, NLPs make it possible for platforms like Cortana and Alexa to respond to requests and know the difference between turn on music, turn up the music, search for music, or buy music.
